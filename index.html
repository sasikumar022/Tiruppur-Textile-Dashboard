EU Over-Dependence Risk
58.2% of exports concentrated in EU. Post-Brexit & EU recession signals create vulnerability. Diversification to US (growing) and ASEAN needed.
Risk Score: HIGH
📈
China+1 Opportunity
US share grew from 12.1% (2021) to 18.4% (2024) — a 52% jump driven by China+1 sourcing shift. Bangladesh & Vietnam are key competitors.
US CAGR: +14.2%
📅
Peak Season: Oct–Feb
Oct–Feb accounts for 62% of annual export volume. Inventory & raw material procurement should begin 3 months prior in Jul–Sep.
62% Revenue in 5 months
📉
GST Disruption Recovery
GST implementation (Jul 2017) caused an 18-month recovery period. Input tax credit delays disrupted SME cash flows across the supply chain.
18-month recovery lag
💱
Currency Competitiveness
Correlation of INR depreciation vs export value = +0.72. Weaker INR boosts competitiveness. FY24 avg rate of ₹83.2/$ is favorable.
Correlation: r = +0.72
🏭
COVID Recovery Milestone
FY2020 saw a 31% crash. Full recovery to pre-COVID levels achieved in FY2022 — 2 years. FY2024 exports are 58% above FY2019 levels.
+58% vs Pre-COVID

Portfolio Project Methodology
Data Sources: Ministry of Textiles texmin.nic.in · DGCI&S dgciskol.gov.in · RBI DBIE · UN Comtrade
1
Data Extraction & Cleaning
Python Pandas requests
  • Scrape DGCI&S monthly export PDFs → CSV
  • Standardize HS codes for knitwear (HS 61xx)
  • Impute COVID gaps with interpolation
  • Merge RBI forex rate data by month
  • Currency normalize to USD for global comparison
2
SQL Analysis Layer
PostgreSQL CTEs Window Fn
  • Rank destinations by 5Y CAGR (RANK() OVER)
  • Pivot: month × year export matrix
  • YoY growth with LAG() function
  • Flag quarters below pre-GST baseline
  • Category-wise revenue share by year
3
Python EDA & Modeling
Seaborn statsmodels scipy
  • Seasonality heatmap (pivot_table + seaborn)
  • Pearson correlation: INR/USD vs exports
  • OLS regression: GDP growth → export volume
  • Time-series decomposition (seasonal_decompose)
  • Anomaly detection on COVID dip period

Power BI Dashboard
Power BI DAX Power Query
